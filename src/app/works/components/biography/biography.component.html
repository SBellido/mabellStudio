Sebastián is a talented graphic designer with 20 years of experience creating several and varied digital solutions. Skilled at software development, he specializes in UX/UI with Adobe XD, Figma, Invision among other technologies. For Sebastián, clean and organized design as well as user experience are essential. He has extensive experience working with testing, user profiles, and information architecture, among other models, methods, and technologies of UX/UI. As a graphic designer he successfully manages sales strategies, digital marketing, brand design, digital publications, packaging design, iIllustrations, editorial design, photography and preparation of sheets for printing.

As a front-end developer, he is skilled at developing and implementing web applications using Angular, JavaScript, Firebase, HTML5 and CSS3.

Sebastián is well versed at communicating with technical and non-technical staff. He is passionate about design and new technologies, constantly seeking to improve his skills. His experience allows him to be involved in the entire design and development process, from planning to implementation.
